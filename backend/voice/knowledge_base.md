# Shifa Health Clinic — Voice Assistant Knowledge Base

## About Us
Shifa Health Clinic is a modern primary-care and telehealth clinic focused on accessible, friendly healthcare. Founded in 2016, our team includes family physicians, nurses, and specialists. We have cared for over 20,000 patients and hold a 4.9-star average rating. We offer both in-person visits and secure video consultations.

## Location & Contact
We are located at 120 Wellness Avenue, Suite 200, Springfield. Free patient parking is available, and the clinic is wheelchair accessible. You can reach us by phone at (555) 020-7788 or by email at care@shifahealth.example. We are a 3-minute walk from the Central Metro station.

## Opening Hours
- Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM
- Sunday: Closed
Telehealth video consultations are available during all opening hours. Same-day urgent slots are reserved each weekday morning between 8:00 AM and 9:30 AM.

## Services Offered
- **General Consultation**: check-ups, common illnesses, and ongoing care with a family physician.
- **Telehealth**: secure video or phone consultations from home.
- **Health Check-ups**: annual physicals and preventive screening packages.
- **Lab Tests**: blood work, diagnostics, and results review.
- **Vaccinations**: flu shots, travel vaccines, and routine immunizations.
- **Chronic Care**: management of diabetes, blood pressure, and other long-term conditions.
- **Specialist Referrals**: cardiology, dermatology, and more via our partner network.
- **Mental Wellness**: counseling and mental-health support sessions.

## Insurance & Payment
We accept most major health insurance plans, including Aetna, Cigna, Blue Cross, and UnitedHealthcare. For patients without insurance, we offer a membership plan at $30 per month that includes two consultations and 15% off all services. We accept cash and all major credit cards, and offer interest-free payment plans for packages over $300.

## Appointments & Telehealth
Appointments can be booked by phone, online, or through our voice assistant. For telehealth, you receive a secure video link by text and email before your visit. We send a reminder 24 hours in advance. Please have any current medications and past reports ready for your consultation.

## Cancellation Policy
Appointments can be rescheduled or cancelled with at least 12 hours notice at no charge. Missed appointments without notice may incur a $20 fee. We do our best to accommodate same-day urgent requests.

## Common Questions (FAQ)
- **Do you offer video consultations?** Yes, telehealth video visits are available during all opening hours for $40.
- **How soon can I be seen?** We reserve same-day urgent slots each weekday morning; routine visits are usually available within 1 to 2 days.
- **Do I need to be a registered patient?** No, we welcome new patients. Your first visit includes a short registration.
- **Do you do lab tests?** Yes, we offer on-site blood work and diagnostics, with results reviewed by a physician.
- **Can I get my prescriptions renewed?** Yes, prescription reviews and renewals can be handled during a consultation.
- **Are you accepting new patients?** Yes, we are currently accepting new patients for both in-person and telehealth visits.

## Important Note
This assistant helps with clinic information and booking appointments only. It does not provide medical diagnoses, prescriptions, or emergency advice. For medical emergencies, patients should call their local emergency number.
